About Us

Team Turco

Unlock your full potential- on and off the field.

Team Turco is a dynamic sports agency dedicated to managing and empowering athletes on and off the field, from a mental support until a fitness assitance at every stage of their careers. We specialize in representing talented individuals across various sports, providing personalized support in training management, fitness assistance, and career planning.

Our Trainers

Diogo Turco

Founder and CEO

Born and raised in Rio de Janeiro, Diogo played in several clubs during his youth career mainly in RIo de Janeiro. Always been sports driven, Diogo studied physical education in the UERJ (University of the State of Rio)

After cooperating with different athletes in different levels and ages, the idea to create TEAM TURCO came across his mind in a in a way to provide full assistance on and off the field, creating a sustainable network between driven athletes enhancing their connections and performances.

Marcelo Cabral

Trainer and Retired football player

Marcelo Cabral is retired football player with an extensive curriculum playing in several clubs in Rio and other states. In the final bit of his career, Marcelo had international experiences in Vietnam, Thailand and United States.

Marcelo was one of the pioneers TEAM TURCO athlete. After coworking for a decade with Diogo, after retiring Marcelo dedicated himself to get his degree as a PT, providing technical and practical experience to other athletes as he was one day.

Our Mission

At Team Turco, our mission is to empower athletes to thrive both on and off the field. We are committed to providing holistic support—from physical training and career guidance to mental and emotional well-being. Through personalized programs and an athlete-first approach, we aim to create lasting impact and elevate performance at every stage of an athlete’s journey.

Our Goals

To nurture a strong network of passionate athletes driven by purpose and excellence.

To offer top-tier physical training, recovery programs, and mental performance coaching.

To support athletes in building sustainable careers, ensuring they grow both professionally and personally.

To expand our global presence, connecting athletes with opportunities beyond borders.

Our Values

Commitment – We are fully dedicated to our athletes' growth, both on and off the field.

Integrity – We believe in honest relationships, transparency, and ethical sportsmanship.

Excellence – We push boundaries to deliver the highest standards in athlete management and support.

Unity – We foster a culture of teamwork, trust, and respect across all disciplines.

Innovation – We embrace new ideas, technologies, and methods to continually improve our approach.
